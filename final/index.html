Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide details of what the temple suites&inn does and what services it provides. people will also be able to make a reservation on the website and see all the needed information about amenities, location, cost and etc.

Target Audience

Persona 1:

They and their family are travelling and planning to see one of the LDS temples and would prefer to stay not far away from it. They love the temple and would be happy to see it from their hotel window. They are also looking for a budget-friendly option and would need somebody to watch their children while they're out

Persona 2:

They are a missionary of the LDS church and are looking for an affordable place to stay near the temple while they're in the city
