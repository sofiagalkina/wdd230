Target Audience

Persona One is a local business owner. They could be male or female, 18 and older wanting to advertise their business on our website. They are looking for any contact information and information about becoming a member of the chamber.

Persona Two is a local resident. It doesn't matter whether they're a male or a female. They are looking for local events, news, and activities they could take part in in the nearest future. Our visitor is most likely between the age of 17 and 40.
