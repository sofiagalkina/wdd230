WEDDINGS

Take your favorite ideas and make them your own at the Temple Inn & Suites. When you host your wedding with us, our community of experts is committed to planning with you, from picking the perfect venue, to orchestrating the menu, to managing day-of logistics.  Whether your dream is an intimate farm-to-table dinner outdoors or a ballroom full of loved ones dancing the night away, we have the spaces, the experience and the skill to give your vision life. We are happy to help you bring all of your family and friends together for an experience they will never forget. After the reception, continue celebrating on the vacation you’ve been dreaming of at one of our many lavish locations. When you celebrate your special day at The Temple hotels, you can earn free nights for your honeymoon.
