Contrast

United Traders

This website is a good representation of what contrast websites look like. It has a lot of contrasting colors alltogether, different companies logos throughout their page and etc. They also use lot of red and white

Alignment

Diesel Forum

This is actually a popular forum in the country i'm coming from. It's design is old yet super simple and makes a lot of sense. Everything is aligned and you can guess what the rest of the website looks like. It's pretty intuitive

Hick's Law

DHgate

This website is a hot mess and one of the best examples of Hick's Law. Everything here is random, you have ads popping up on different categories items, constant discount offers and email sign-up windows. It has so many categories!
